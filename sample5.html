About:

I took up Digital Design & Illustration in 2016 as a personal outlet. Day after day, I continue learning and honing my Photoshop, Figma and Canva skills. This outlet help me to think creatively, solve problems, be meticulous, and work with software often used in this field. The knowledge and abilities I've gained can be applied to different aspects.

Process:

1. Conceptualized the ideas by collecting requirements or taking inspiration from the world around me.
2. Drew a rough sketch
3. Created the work using Figma or Photoshop and Canva.
4. Improve the piece and added final details.
5. Reflected the procedure and noted areas that needed improvement for upcoming initiatives.
6. The end results (below)

Future Goals:

1. Become accredited in Adobe Premier Pro and CapCut
2. Learn Procreate
