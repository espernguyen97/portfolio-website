Project Details

(Covid-19 Data Analysis)

About Dataset:

This project data was provided by Our World In Data , here was given the dataset contains the confirmed COVID-19 cases, deaths, testing and vaccinations from 2020 to 2022. There are 2 Excel files, one with total deaths, and the other one with data for total vaccinations. All the data can be downloaded on the website or through the link below.

Goal:

Analyzing data using SQL and Tableau to measure and track global COVID-19 cases and deaths by world region

What are the tasks ?

Through the data given, I am looking for:
Task 1: The total cases, total death, global death percentage.
Task 2: The total death in different continents.
Task 3: The percentage of population infected in different countries.
Task 4: The percentage of population infected per month in different countries.
Task 5: A dashboard of the charts made for all tasks above.

Conclusion:

The total cases are 150,574,977 with the countries with the highest share of COVID-19 cases worldwide included India, Brazil, the United Kingdom, the United States with US' cases are around 10 percent of total cases worldwide and this number tends to increase to 20 percent of total global cases. Out of 150,574,977 cases, the Covid-19 deaths accounts for 2.11%. The number of total death is highest in Europe and the second position belongs to North America while the least one is in Oceania. In terms of the total death per country, it can be seen that both the number of total cases worldwide and the number of total death worldwide are highest in the United States.
