Project Details

(Spotify Music Classification and Personalization)

About Dataset:

The dataset consists 2018 rows and contains all data retrieved from Spotify, which includes the data of Music Acousticness, Music Danceability, Duration_ms, Energy, Music Instrumentalnes, Music Key, Music Liveness, Loudness, Mode, Music Speechless,Music Tempo, Time_Signature, Valence, Target, Song Title, Artist The data is openly available on Google Drive: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1wxT6whlSMTP4eYefggIlkgNBte2k443S?usp=sharing or it can be downloaded through the link below.

Goal:

Using Tableau to explore the components of music such as Acousticness, Liveness, Speechiness, Instrumentalness and Danceability and how Spotify music is classified based off the data provided

What are the tasks given?

Through the data given, I'm looking for:
Task 1: Music Classification
Task 2: Music Acousticness
Task 3: Music Liveness
Task 4: Music Speechiness
Task 5: Music Instrumentalness
Task 6: Music Danceability
Task 7: Artist Segmentation
Task 8: Preferred Artist
Task 9: A dashboard of the charts made for all tasks above.
