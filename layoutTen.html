Project Details

(San Francisco International Airport (SFO)'s Airline Data Analysis)

About Dataset:

This project data was retrieved from Air Travel Open Dataset, here was given the dataset contains geometry coordinates, date, flight numbers, airlines, in 2020. The data is openly available on Google Drive: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/19ENhHT_2-cyOSdBRotJO8uPM2tkN4yEp?usp=sharing" or it can be downloaded through the link below.

Goal:

Visualizing and analyzing the historical data at SFO using Tableau in order to track, measure the impact of COVID-19 on SFO airport

What are the tasks given?

Through the data given, I am looking for:
Task 1: The total number of flights at San Francisco International Airport.
Task 2: The number of flights taking off per day in March and then find the busiest day at San Francisco International Airport.
Task 3: The total number of flights per day in a month at San Francisco International Airport.
Task 4: The top 10 busiest flight routes.
Task 5: The top 10 longest flight routes.
Task 6: The flights from San Francisco International Airport to different parts of the world.
Task 7: A dashboard of the charts and map made for all tasks above.

Conclusion:

In 2020, the total number of flights was 88532 with the busiest routes was from SFO to LAX and the longest routes was from SFO to DUB. The number of total flights from SFO to LAX was 7968, which was almost twice higher than the second busiest routes (SFO - JFK) while the least busiest one belonged to SFO - BOS. In terms of international destinations, the number of flights from SFO to Europe and South America were the highest. When it came to the number of flights in March, the total flights taking off each day did not fluctuate much in the first half of the month with the busiest day at San Francisco International Airport was the sixth of March with 3295 flights. However, due to Covid-19 outbreak, the total flights per day decline drastically when reaching the end of the month, which led to the heavy loss of revenues
