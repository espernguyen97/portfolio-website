Project Details

(Sales Analysis)

About dataset:

The dataset contains the sales report in the last two years (2020 - 2021) consist 286393 rows, which included information about all customers, Order status, Discount information, Product category, Price, Oder quantity, Payment method, Order date. All the data are openly available and they can be downloaded on the website or through the link below.

Goal:

Based off the historical data, analyzing the sales of a company and visualizing data using Tableau in order to measure, monitor the sales as well as recommend approaches to improve the business efficiency

What are the tasks given?

Through the data given, I'm looking for:
Task 1: The company revenue per US state.
Task 2: The monthly revenue of the company.
Task 3: Age distribution of customers.
Task 4: The relationship between the discount on products and the quantity of customer orders.
Task 5: The company revenue in different US regions.
Task 6: Gender distribution of customers per product category.
Task 7: The total revenue of the company.
Task 8: A dashboard of the charts and map made for all tasks above.

Conclusion:

The company had a total revenue of $233,650,194. The discount on products had affected the order quantity since as the discounts increased, it stimulated large orders from customers. The sales fluctuated from January until October with the highest revenue of 33.7 in March. Since October, the revenues had risen sharply and peaked when reaching the last two months of the year. Most big states like California, Texas, New York, Pennsylvania and Florida witnessed higher revenue than other states with the highest sales of $13.9 mil was in California.
The age distribution of customers showed that the 30s contributed the most revenue and the 10s accounted for the least amount of revenues. The 20s, 40s, 50s, 60s together also made up the majority of the revenues. Mobiles & Tablets was the top interest for both gender, while the second position belonged to appliances and the third most popular was entertainment. Customers seemed to be less interest in Books and School & Education product category when their sales were only approximately 0.2 mil and 0.6 mil respectively in comparison to other product categories.
