Project Details

(Sales Analysis)

Overview:

Objectives:

Create Tableau charts and the dashboard to analyze the sales of a US company:
Task 1: Create a map that represents the company revenue per US state.
Task 2: Create a line chart that depicts the monthly revenue of the company.
Task 3: Create a bar chart that shows age distribution of customers.
Task 4: Create a scatter plot that shows the relationship between the discount on products and the quantity of customer orders.
Task 5: Create a chart that illustrates the company revenue in different US regions.
Task 6: Create a bar chart that depicts gender distribution of customers per product category.
Task 7: A visualization that shows the total revenue of the company.
Task 8: Create a dashboard of the charts and map made for all tasks above.
