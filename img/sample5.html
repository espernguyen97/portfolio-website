Design & Illustration



I took up Digital design & Illustration in 2016 as a personal outlet. Day after day, I continue to learn and hone my skills in Photoshop, Figma and Canva. This outlet helped me to think creatively, solve problems, be meticulous, and work with software that is often used in this field. The knowledge and abilities I've gained can be applied to project management, marketing communications strategy, and content marketing.

The Process

1. Conceptualised the ideas by drawing inspiration the world around me.
2. Drew a rough sketch or initial wireframes.
3. Created the work using Figma, Photoshop, Canva.
4. Improve the piece and added final details.
5. Reflected the procedure and noted areas that needed improvement for upcoming initiatives.
