INTRODUCTION:

To busy but health-conscious people who cannot maintain a proper oral hygiene routine and always wish for an electric toothbrush that is smart, fast, eco-friendly and effective, our product UNOBRUSH is the optimal solution for them. We believe that UNOBRUSH understands what our customers need and provides them what they want. With that in mind, our enduring mission is “ Produce the high-end toothbrushes, cause no unnecessary damage to the environment, use business to inspire and drive us toward a more sustainable world.


PRODUCT DESCRIPTIONS:

UNOBRUSH™ is the world’s most astute toothbrush that delivers a refreshing clean to your mouth, teeth, and gums.
It combines an:
+ ergonomic mouthpiece
+ patent-pending 360-degree wrapping UNOfoam™
+ pulsing sonic vibrations
Enabling it to remove the plaque by reaching 99.9% of the surfaces and crevices on your teeth – cleaning your whole mouth in just 6 seconds.
The UNOBRUSH is broken down into three parts - UNOfoam, Connector & Base Module (Handle) and it also comes along with a docking station . We planned the UNOBRUSH™ docking station to disinfect the mouthpiece with UV light after every usage, forestalling the development of destructive microscopic organisms that bargain the wellbeing of your teeth and gums.
The UNOBRUSH has the easiest and fastest procedure of brushing your teeth with just 4 basic steps as follows:
STEP 1: Place the UNOBRUSH into your mouth and turn it on.
STEP 2: Wait for six seconds.
STEP 3: Take it out of your mouth and Rinse.
STEP 4: Done! Put it back into the Docking station.
The core features of the UNOBRUSH are as per the following:
1. UNOfoam™
Through broad R&D, clinical preliminaries, research and many material tests, we created UNOfoam™, our patent-pending medical-grade sponge-like material that is enhanced explicitly for oral cleanliness. UNOfoam™ is designed from high-density polyurethane and implanted with micro-ridges coded in a criss-cross pattern to create micro-cleaning friction effective enough to evacuate 99.9% of dental plaque and sufficiently fragile to delicately invigorate and shield your gums from the long haul threats of gingival inflammation. Its remarkable cell structure and thickness at the same time transfer vibrations while remaining flexible enough to shape and conform to every tooth, coupling 360-degree Wrap™ technology and intense sonic development – a progressive blending unheard of in the oral care industry that attempts to lift the oral plaque from your teeth surface and stimulate the gums.
2. 360° Wrap™ To guarantee UNOfoam™ could fit each mouth, we innovated a 3D tooth mapping™ technique to accomplish the mission of creating a one-size-fits-all mouthpiece that reaches 99.9% of surfaces and crevices regardless of the size or shape of your mouth. The UNOfoam™’s structure not only forms into each mouth but also wraps around every tooth to clean distal and buccal surfaces simultaneously.
3. Fluid wave™ The fluid wave technology along with combination of UNOfoam™ and sonic vibrations ensures the flow of toothpaste between every tooth and below the gum line, so that it reaches into place that a normal manual toothbrush fails to clean.

COMPETITORS IN THE MARKET :

Since it is a fairly expensive product, it competes with high-end electric toothbrushes produced by popular companies such as Oral B, and Philips Sonicare. These toothbrushes are widely available and produced by trusted brands. UNOBRUSH is supposed to be an improved and more efficient version of these toothbrushes.
UNOBRUSH can also compete with more niche toothbrushes. There are products that resemble more traditional electric toothbrushes like Foreo and Gum but are from smaller brands and have cult followings. There are also products like the Smile 30 toothbrushes which are a significant improvement from traditional toothbrushes. Smile 30 brushes the upper and lower teeth at the same time which greatly reduces the recommended brushing time.
UNOBRUSH ’s main competitors are other mouthpiece toothbrushes like Amabrush, hibrush, and Unico. Though none of these use the exact same technology as unobrush, they target the same market segment.

SWOT ANALYSIS:

Strengths:
One of the greatest features of UNOBRUSH is the time it takes for a complete brushing of the teeth. Unlike a traditional or electric toothbrush, each of which takes about 2 minutes (1. How long should I brush my teeth?), UNOBRUSH takes only 6 seconds (UNOBRUSH – Tooth Brushing Reimagined). This feature is extremely appealing in today’s busy lifestyle. People in different walks in life, like professionals or students, are always looking to save time.
The health benefits of UNOBRUSH are significant. The main function of a toothbrush is for health purposes. First, UNOBRUSH cleans all of the teeth, while a traditional toothbrush may cover only 40-80% of the teeth surface area (UNOBRUSH – Tooth Brushing Reimagined). One cause for the low coverage of the traditional toothbrush is the general lack of compliance to the dentists’ instructions on tooth brushing technique. Next, the use of UV light technology to sanitize Toothbrush makes the product adds to the health and oral hygiene benefit of the users (UNOBRUSH – Tooth Brushing Reimagined). Good dental health also reduces the risk of other health problems including heart diseases and diabetes.
UNOBRUSH’s portability is another attractive aspect. That is, the small-sized Unobrush with a full charge which can be used for two weeks (UNOBRUSH FAQ) can be carried around so that users can use it almost anywhere anytime. The portable nature is especially beneficial to professionals who can maintain good and clean hygiene throughout the day despite their busy schedules. The portability with the compact design and size makes Unobrush ideal for travelers as well.
Weaknesses:
+ The credibility of the inventors is a weakness. Founders Daniel Kristofferson and Andreas Dierks were 19-20 years old when they launched UNOBRUSH (Margolis). At such a young age, they do not have much business education or experience. They also have not had the opportunity to study dental health (Margolis). Their lack of appropriate background can seriously undermine their ability to sell an innovative product.
+ The lack of published clinical trials (Margolis) questions UNOBRUSH as an effective and reliable health product. People do not want to use a health product that does not perform its intended function properly. Data from a large scale clinical trial is not available.
+ There is an ongoing costs after a purchase of Unobrush. In addition to the expensive retail purchase price of over $200 US, users are required to spend $3 US per month to replace the foam (UNOBRUSH – Tooth Brushing Reimagined). Overall, the cost of using Unobrush is much higher than using a traditional toothbrush. It is also higher than an electric toothbrush. Next, the warranty is one year (UNOBRUSH FAQ). Without knowing how long the product will last, there may be other parts which may need to be replaced after one year. The replacement costs for those parts are unknown, but they will add to the cost of continuing to use the product.
Opportunities:
+ The toothbrush has not changed much over the years. There is an opportunity for a revolutionary product which Unobrush can provide. The electric toothbrush is a motorized version of the traditional toothbrush. Besides, the electric toothbrush still needs about the same amount of time for use. However, UNOBRUSH is fundamentally a different product in terms of function and performance that can dramatically change the oral hygiene market.
+ It is well known that children do not enjoy brushing their teeth. In addition, they have yet to develop the habit of brushing their teeth. Many often do not brush their teeth or spend enough time brushing their teeth. Consequently, many children suffer from tooth decay, which is the most common childhood health problem according to American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry (Surprising Oral Health Facts About Children). However, with UNOBRUSH, parents can ensure that their children’s teeth are cleaned fully. Because UNOBRUSH is so easy to use, UNOBRUSH offers an excellent opportunity for parents to develop good dental hygiene practices with their children.
+ Even though UNOBRUSH is an innovative product, there are opportunities for UNOBRUSH to partner with dentists and other dental health professionals for mutual support and benefit. The effectiveness of UNOBRUSH can be tested through thorough clinical trials to prove its effectiveness.
Threats:
+ Manufacturers of traditional and electric toothbrushes can only see UNOBRUSH as a new competitor in the market. They will likely plan for a pushback to protect their market share. One possible tactic is to exploit apparent weaknesses or whatever features of UNOBRUSH they consider negative.
+ If UNOBRUSH becomes successful, there will be other companies that will try to develop similar products. While there are patents to protect Unobrush, there could be companies that disregard patents, produce similar but inferior products, or sell similar products at a much lower price. This is a common trend when an innovative product sells well.
+ As an innovative product, there are risks of quality control, production delays, timely deliveries to customers, and sufficient customer support. With prior experience, it is difficult to project the necessary preparation for various unanticipated challenges and setbacks.


MARKETING MIX:

1. Product strategy:
UNOBRUSH is the toothbrush reinvented. influenced by NASA shape technology, we have created a sleek and easy to use system. The brush is designed for quick and easy use; and once released, it will become an essential utility for the fast-paced life.
UNOBRUSH comes in a kit and each kit includes the following:
+ Connector: connects the unofoam to the base module
+ Base Module: packs the unit which provides the sonic vibrations, also works as a handle
+ UNOfoam: medical grade sponge-like material which cleans the teeth through micro-friction
+ Docking Station: Sanitizes the brush with UV light after every use
As it is not based on the design of a conventional toothbrush, and is something totally new in the market, we have chosen to throw a unique spotlight on the customer experience. The product is designed to these specific criteria:
+ No-mess Technology - the mouthpiece is shaped so as to counteract any dripping onto the garments while the product is being used
+ Travel-Friendly - easy to pack and the toothbrush holds the charge for one week
+ Water resistant - impervious to regular rinsing and splashing
UNOBRUSH utilizes 95% less energy than the standard electric toothbrush henceforth making it the world’s first green smart toothbrush. Not only that, the brush is manufactured under strict-trade standards
UNOBRUSH has been developed and perfected over several years and is made from the best materials and formulas to revolutionize the experience of brushing the teeth. The product is accompanied with a 1-year warranty which incorporates a total replacement if by any chance the product does not work properly
The UNOBRUSH will be released in kits of four variants:
1. UNOBRUSH single: UNOBRUSH Connector, UNOBRUSH base module, docking station, 12x Unofoam
2. UNOBRUSH twin: 2x UNOBRUSH Connector, 2x UNOBRUSH base module, 2x docking station, 12x Unofoam
3. UNOBRUSH family: 4x UNOBRUSH Connector, UNOBRUSH base module, docking station, 12x Unofoam
4. UNOBRUSH hospital: 20x UNOBRUSH Connector, 4x UNOBRUSH base module, 4x docking station, 60x Unofoam
2. Distribution strategy:
Dentists who essentially act as ‘opinion leaders’ will play a huge role in early marketing and distribution of the product. This product could be recommended to people who have a fast-paced life and don’t have the time or the energy to brush twice a day. From a point of view for a niche market, the product can be used by people who have lack manual dexterity
+ As this is a new product so the places of distribution will be the ones which are easily accessible for all potential customers.
+ Products will be available in major supermarkets,wholesale warehouses, discount department stores like Superstore, Costco, Sobeys, Loblaws, Walmart, Metro, NoFrill etc.
+ Company outlets will be launched in major cities, with pop-ups in cities around the country. More stores will be opened as the product gains traction
+ A Subscription based model for ordering UNOfoam will be released through the website so that the customers do not have to worry about running out of their monthly supply. For keeping a regular chain of supply, we will be partnered with logistic companies which deliver the UNOfoam to the doorstep every month.
3. Promotional strategy:
As the product is new, it needs to attract as many consumers as possible so we are trying to put the product on the map anyhow we can.
Initially, the budget for our advertisement will be less compared to other popular established companies like Oral B, but as our company earns more profit, the budget for advertising will increase. We are going with the affordable budget method due to budget constraints.
Since our products are new, our advertising objective is informative advertising in order to explain how UNOBRUSH works and assist customers when they first use our toothbrushes with the new technology, persuade consumers that UNOBRUSH offer the best toothbrush for their money, and most importantly, communicate with our potential customers in the market.
As mentioned in the distribution strategy, dentists as ‘opinion leaders’ play an important role in early marketing. Our target customers really care about health and dental issues so they definitely go to dental clinics and hospitals. We can cooperate with these dentists to promote and distribute our products. After each dental cleaning or other dental cares, when dental hygienists give their patients advice about how to maintain oral health, they can recommend our products and give their patients our 50% discount vouchers. Also. we will create our product introduction video and let it play and repeat in the areas where most people are waiting for their dental care in dental clinics and hospitals.
Our target customers are also the young including universities students, graduates so we focus on promoting in the places where they can see and easily access to our products. Take York Fest at York University for instance. York Fest is a traditional event running every September during the welcome week, where many clubs, organizations and companies on and off campus do tabling and promote their brands, products and services along the campus walk. 48,000+ students are expected to participate in this event so we can take advantage of these fairs and events like this.
Once again, we will play our product introduction video and create some mini games or quizzes relevant to our products, our mission and our video. Of course, who wants to win our games have to see our videos. This will help to remind our customers of our products and brand. Also, on the day the event or the fair occurs, we offer free dental care that are operated by the dentists we cooperate, and we continue to give our 50% discount vouchers for those who come to our table, promote and buy our products or participate in our quizzes and games. Now we can only tabling in some top universities in big cities but once our company earns more profit, we will expand our scale and operations to other universities in remote areas
In major supermarkets, wholesale warehouses, discount department stores where we distribute our products, we hand out our 50% discount vouchers and let those who come to our booth try our products for free. Our promotion counter will be running only every 4 months because of our budget constraints.
We also know that our target customers are now living in the digital world. At this moment, our products can be seen on Yahoo and Radio-Canada but for effective social media marketing and better communication as well as connection with our valuable customers, we work our best to promote our products and brands to most potential social media platforms such as Google+, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and Youtube. In addition, we do follow social media trends and we are willing to cooperate with the influencers to both inspire and expand reach as well as bring our customers the best engagement.
Various other tools will also be used along with the stated above, we will be setting a Public relation team which will help the company to handle its public more efficiently.
4. Pricing Strategy:
As pricing is the most important part of the marketing mix as it gives return to company and it should be strategized keeping many things in mind and it’s not an easy task to do.At the same time, we have to compete with other popular standard brands that rule the market for so long and prevent new rivals from entering the market. Here, we are using MARKET- SKIMMING PRICING so that new competitors will not be able to enter the market easily. Not everyone can be successful in using this method as the price will be high compared to our competitors, so the quality of our product should be the only thing which is the only tool of differentiation between ours and our competitors. The most important thing we have to keep in mind while using this strategy is if our consumers are willing to pay the price which we are setting. In other words, it should be affordable to a large group of markets even though the prices are high.
We know that our products are still new in this market and the initial price is a little bit high to our customers so discount and allowance pricing are the best choices at this early stage. We offer a 50% discount and reduce prices to reward our valuable customers when they pay early or promote our products. We will also use captive product pricing as we will be selling the product in a bundle along with the main product like UNOBRUSH connector, UNOBRUSH base module, docking station, and 3X Unofoam in the future.
